What is
Monova?

Web3 platform

Monova is a Web3 platform for real estate stakeholders. It helps users connect
and manage physical and digital assets using unique, shared 3D models of
their buildings.

The Digital Twin Token: The building’s unique digital copy

Monova uses blockchain technology to record essential data, which enables
transparent, traceable, and immutable streams of information to be readily
accessible to its users. Monova smart contracts are built with this blockchain
data to expedite tasks like requests for proposals, lease agreements, and
services arrangements.

An Index of digitization

The Monova Index helps with decision-making by offering insights on data sharing,
creating services, and meeting operational or environmental goals.
